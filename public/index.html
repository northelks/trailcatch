Welcome to TrailCatch!

TrailCatch is the perfect app for anyone who loves outdoor activities and enjoys them with their furry friends. Whether you're walking, running or biking, the app provides detailed analytics and a unique social experience to keep you motivated and engaged.

TrailCatch is more than just an app; it's a comprehensive companion designed for both you and your dogs. TrailCatch is your ultimate companion for walking, running or biking adventures. With user-friendly features, detailed analytics, leaderboards, and a points system, the app makes your trail more enjoyable and engaging.

Download TrailCatch on the App Store and start your journey of adventure and fun with your best friend by your side!

Key Feature

Friends Feeds

The Friends Feed page on TrailCatch keeps you connected and inspired by showcasing the latest activities of your friends

Interactive Posts: Like posts to engage with your friends and to stay connected and motivate each other.

Customizable Feed: Filter and customize your feed to see the activities that matter most to you, whether it’s specific types of activities or updates from your closest friends.

Shared Insights: View detailed stats and maps of their activities, providing you with insights and inspiration for your own adventures.

Key Feature

Advanced Filters

TrailCatch offers a robust filtering system to help you find the perfect friend's trails and activities. Customize your search using the following filters:

Type of Trail: Easily find trails suited for walking, running or biking, or dog-friendly adventures.

Gender: Discover activities and trails popular among specific gender groups.

Age Group: Filter trails and activities based on different age groups to match your preferences.

Dog Breed: Locate trails frequented by specific dog breeds, ensuring a great experience for you and your furry friend.

Key Feature

Map Discover

TrailCatch’s interactive map makes discovering new trails a breeze. You can effortlessly explore and locate nearby trails used by both friends and other TrailCatch users. You can find the perfect trail to suit your activity needs and join others in their favorite spots.

You can easily filter and zoom in on trails that match your preferences, see detailed trail information, and get inspired to explore new paths and connect with fellow adventurers.

Key Feature

Activity Monitoring

Walk, Run, Bike: Accurately monitor your distance, elevation, duration, speed/pace and hearth rate data for various activities. TrailCatch helps you monitor your progress and advance your performance.

View your activity data through intuitive and interactive charts. Analyze your results over time, compare different activities, and identify trends to enhance your experience and impressions of outdoor activities.

Key Feature 3

Device Data Sync

Apple Health, Garmin, Suunto, Poland and others integration: Sync your health and activity data from your favourite devices seamlessly, ensuring all your information is consolidated in one place.

TrailCatch offers a streamlined experience for managing your activity data. Easily sync new recorded data from your devices with just a few taps. Whether you're connecting a new device or updating your existing data, TrailCatch ensures a smooth and hassle-free process, keeping your information current and accurate.

Key Feature 4

Leaderboards

Country-Based Leaderboards: Compete with users from around the world and see how you rank in your country. The leaderboards add a fun and competitive element to your activities.

Points for Activities: Earn points for every activity you complete. The more activities you do, the more points you accumulate. Accumulate points through your activities to elevate your standing and showcase your dedication and progress among fellow users of the TrailCatch community.

Key Feature 5

You & Your Dogs

Dog Profiles: Create profiles for your dogs to keep track of their specific needs and activity patterns.

Dog-Friendly Routes: Discover and follow dog-friendly trails and paths, making it easy for dog owners to find and enjoy new adventures with their pets.

Dog Metrics: Monitor your dog's activity levels, including distance walked, playtime, and more, ensuring your furry friends stays healthy and active.

Key Feature 6

Community and Social

Connect with Friends: Join the TrailCatch community to share your achievements, follow your friends' activities, and motivate each other.

Share on Social Media: Easily share your activity summaries and charts on social media platforms to showcase your progress and inspire others.
