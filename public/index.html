Welcome to TrailCatch!

TrailCatch is the perfect app for anyone who loves outdoor activities and enjoys them with their furry friends. Whether you're walking, running or biking, the app provides detailed analytics and a unique social experience to keep you motivated and engaged.

Download TrailCatch from the App Store and embark on a thrilling journey of adventure and fun with your best friend by your side! Discover new trails, connect with fellow outdoor enthusiasts, and make the most of every outing. With TrailCatch, every moment spent exploring together becomes a cherished memory!

Explore, create, and share your custom trails! Build unique graphs of your activity, track your progress, and inspire others. Sync your trails effortlessly with popular fitness devices to capture real-time stats and track your progress with precision. Whether you're analyzing heart rate peaks, checking energy output over challenging terrain, or tracking cadence patterns on long hikes, TrailCatch presents this data in detailed, intuitive charts and visuals.

Devices

Key Feature

GeoLocation

Your privacy comes first. Choose what personal data to show or hide, with complete confidence - especially your GeoLocation, which is always encrypted and never shared with third parties.

Your privacy is our utmost priority. With TrailCatch, you have the power to decide exactly what personal data you want to display or keep private, giving you complete control over your information. Explore, connect, and enjoy your adventures with the confidence that your privacy is always protected!

 

 

Key Feature

Data Protection

Your trail details and geolocation are safeguarded with advanced PGP encryption, ensuring top-level cryptographic privacy for your personal data.

Your trail details and geolocation are meticulously protected with cutting-edge PGP encryption, delivering top-level cryptographic privacy for your personal data. This robust security measure ensures that your information remains confidential and inaccessible to unauthorized parties. With every adventure you embark on, you can trust that your privacy is prioritized, allowing you to focus on the journey ahead.
